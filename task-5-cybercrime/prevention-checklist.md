Title
Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist
Checklist
Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, passwords, or banking details with anyone.
Do not scan unknown QR codes sent by strangers or received through suspicious messages.
Always verify bank-related calls, emails, or payment requests through official channels.
Avoid clicking suspicious links received through SMS, WhatsApp, email, or social media.
Check every UPI payment request carefully before approving any transaction.
Use strong and unique passwords for important accounts and enable two-factor authentication.
Keep your phone, apps, and operating system updated to reduce security risks.
Download apps only from official and trusted sources such as Google Play Store or App Store.
Avoid making financial transactions while connected to public Wi-Fi networks.
Immediately report any suspicious transaction, fraud attempt, or cybercrime incident.
