## Project Overview
This Digital Literacy Project was completed as part of the **CSE0001 – Digital Literacy** course. The aim of this project is to develop essential digital skills required for students in academics and professional life. It covers digital awareness, online safety, communication, coding platforms, professional presence, and cybercrime awareness.

This repository contains all five project tasks along with the final project report and supporting files.

## Task Summaries

### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
Created a one-page infographic explaining the meaning and importance of digital literacy, including safe internet practices, digital tools, professional online presence, and email etiquette.

### Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
Created or updated professional student profiles on digital platforms such as GitHub and LinkedIn to build a professional online identity.

### Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms
Explored coding practice using HackerRank and created a Google Form quiz on digital literacy awareness linked with Google Sheets.

### Task 4 – Professional Email & Etiquette Guide
Drafted two professional emails and created a social media do’s and don’ts checklist for responsible online communication.

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness Case Study & Prevention Guide
Prepared a case study on UPI / online payment fraud and created a prevention checklist for online safety.
