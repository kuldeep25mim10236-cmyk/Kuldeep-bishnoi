# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

## Do’s
- [ ] Use respectful and professional language online.
- [ ] Think before posting, commenting, or sharing content.
- [ ] Keep profile information clean and appropriate.
- [ ] Use privacy settings to protect personal information.
- [ ] Share positive and useful content responsibly.

## Don’ts
- [ ] Do not post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
- [ ] Do not share personal or financial information publicly.
- [ ] Do not spread fake news or unverified information.
- [ ] Do not engage in cyberbullying or unnecessary online conflicts.
- [ ] Do not upload anything that can harm your reputation.
